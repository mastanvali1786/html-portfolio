MY FAVOURITE MOIVES

Inception (2010)

A mind-bending sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan, where a skilled thief, Dom Cobb, specializes in stealing secrets from people's subconscious during dreams. Tasked with planting an idea instead of stealing one, Cobb and his team navigate a dangerous maze of dreams within dreams.

The Pursuit of Happyness (2006)

A heartwarming drama based on a true story, starring Will Smith as Chris Gardner, a struggling salesman who becomes homeless while caring for his young son. Despite relentless challenges, Chris's determination leads him to secure a life-changing internship at a prestigious brokerage firm.

Parasite (2019)

A dark comedy and thriller directed by Bong Joon-ho that explores class disparities in South Korea. The story follows a poor family that infiltrates a wealthy household by posing as unrelated, highly qualified workers, leading to unexpected and devastating consequences.
